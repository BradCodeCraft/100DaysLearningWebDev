My name is Thien An Nguyen, but I am also known as Brad Nguyen. I am an undergraduate majoring in Information Systems with a minor in Computer Science at Kelley School of Business of Indiana University. I was born and raised in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam most of my life.

When I was 17, I was fortunate enough to be given an opportunity to study abroad in the United States. I boarded an episcopal school in Boca Raton, Florida named Saint Andrew's School. It was there where I went on to learn about the American cultures, religions, and so on. I developed an interest for Business through a basic economic course, AP Microeconomics, and AP Macroeconomics. Nonetheless, I had a feeling compelling me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in a field intersecting Business and Technology.

It was not in my vision that I would attend Kelley School of Business or Indiana University, but Got has led me here for a purpose. I struggled to fit in during my first year at Indiana University as I do not know where I would fit in. However, God showed me Salt Company, and my journey as a Christian started there. I got baptised on December fourth of 2022, applied and became a leader, Strike Team leader, for Salt.

I have shown an interest in Information Technology and Softwares since I was in middle school. I would spend most of my time learning about computer through the help of YouTube. I learned about computer softwares and hardwares. Additionally, my curiosity for computer softwares had empowered me to begin my journey in programming. My first programming language was JavaScript; however, due to school, I did not spend much time learning and perfecting my programming skill.
